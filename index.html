About Me

I am an international educator with more than a decade of experience across primary education, teacher development, instructional quality, and educational leadership. My teaching journey has taken me from India to China, where I have had the privilege of working with multilingual learners in bilingual school environments.

My classroom is built on the belief that every child can succeed when learning is engaging, purposeful, and supported through meaningful relationships. I strive to create an environment where students feel confident to communicate, think critically, make mistakes, and grow as independent learners.

Alongside classroom teaching, I have worked in teacher training, quality assurance, and instructional coaching. These experiences have strengthened my ability to design effective learning experiences, mentor colleagues, analyse student progress, and continuously improve teaching practice through reflection and research.

I believe that great teaching is a balance of compassion, professionalism, evidence-informed practice, and lifelong learning. Through this portfolio, I hope to share my journey, my classroom, and the values that continue to shape my work as an educator.
