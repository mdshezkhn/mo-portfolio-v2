# Recruiter Objection Map

This document maps the primary psychological and professional objections a Recruiter or Head of School might have when reviewing the portfolio, cross-references them against the existing evidence, and identifies areas where further proof is needed.

---

### International Experience
- **Objection:** Do they understand the international school context, or are they just a local teacher trying to transition?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Career Journey outlines 10 years in Zhengzhou bilingual international schools.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** Include context in the CV about the specific demographic of students (e.g., host-country nationals vs. expats).
- **Status:** **Adequate**

### Classroom Management
- **Objection:** Can they manage behavior in a dynamic, primary classroom setting?
- **Evidence Already Present:** 10 years in primary education.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** A classroom video demonstrating calm, authoritative, and positive behavioral management.
- **Status:** **Future evidence recommended.**

### EAL Expertise
- **Objection:** Are they equipped to teach students who do not speak English as a first language?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Explicit mention of translanguaging theory and TEFL/TESOL certifications.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** A photo in the gallery showing scaffolding (e.g., a bilingual word wall or graphic organizer).
- **Status:** **Adequate, but gallery evidence recommended.**

### STEM Integration
- **Objection:** Do they genuinely know STEM, or is it just a buzzword?
- **Evidence Already Present:** B.Sc in Physics provides strong subject-matter foundation.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** Gallery photos showing active, hands-on STEM learning (e.g., measuring, building, or observing experiments).
- **Status:** **Future evidence recommended.**

### Technology Integration
- **Objection:** Can they adapt to modern school ed-tech ecosystems?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Mention of utilizing LMS data analytics to monitor teaching effectiveness at WhiteHat Jr.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** Mention of specific tools (e.g., Seesaw, Canvas, Google Classroom) in the CV.
- **Status:** **Adequate**

### Leadership Potential
- **Objection:** Are they capable of stepping up if a middle-leadership role opens?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Mentoring early-career teachers, quality-assurance experience with 200+ educators, delivering international PD.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** A recommendation letter from a current Principal attesting to leadership capacity.
- **Status:** **Adequate**

### Curriculum Knowledge
- **Objection:** Do they know the UK National Curriculum, PYP, or Common Core?
- **Evidence Already Present:** PGCE from a UK university.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** Explicit mention of the specific curricula taught (e.g., Cambridge Primary, IPC) in the CV.
- **Status:** **Adequate (relying on CV to fill the gap).**

### Professional Development
- **Objection:** Do they stay current with educational research?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Recent completion of PGCE (2026), practitioner research on dialogic teaching, continuous PD listed in Credentials.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** None needed.
- **Status:** **Strong**

### Reflective Practice
- **Objection:** Are they open to feedback and self-improvement?
- **Evidence Already Present:** The entire "Teaching Philosophy" and "Practitioner Research" sections are built on reflection.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** None needed.
- **Status:** **Strong**

### Stability
- **Objection:** Will they break contract or leave after one year?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Maintained a sustained presence in Zhengzhou (10 years) rather than jumping between countries annually.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** None needed.
- **Status:** **Strong**

### Communication
- **Objection:** Can they write and speak at the level expected of an international educator?
- **Evidence Already Present:** The portfolio itself serves as proof of written communication (M.A. in English Language & Literature also supports this).
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** The teaching video will serve as proof of spoken communication.
- **Status:** **Future evidence recommended (Video).**

### Qualifications
- **Objection:** Will they pass our HR and visa compliance checks?
- **Evidence Already Present:** PGCE, B.Ed, B.Sc, M.A., TEFL, TESOL all listed with thumbnails.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** Ensure the CV explicitly states "Available for verification" and all degree scans are clear when requested.
- **Status:** **Adequate**

### Cross-Cultural Competence
- **Objection:** Can they thrive in a diverse staff room and respect local culture?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Mentions of working with international teams (UK, Dubai, Malta) and 10 years living in China.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** None needed.
- **Status:** **Strong**

### Safeguarding Awareness
- **Objection:** Do they understand modern child protection protocols?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Currently missing from the portfolio explicitly.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** A dedicated bullet point in the CV or a certificate (e.g., Educare/NSPCC) in the Professional Development section.
- **Status:** **Future evidence recommended.**

### Parent Communication
- **Objection:** Can they handle demanding international school parents?
- **Evidence Already Present:** Currently missing.
- **Recommended Supporting Evidence:** A bullet point in the CV detailing parent-teacher conferences, newsletters, or reporting frameworks.
- **Status:** **Future evidence recommended.**
