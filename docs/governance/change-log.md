## Measurable Success Criteria

| Stage              | Metric          | Why it matters                                                                                             |
| ------------------ | --------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Applications       | Response rate   | Measures whether your CV + LinkedIn + portfolio combination gets attention.                                |
| Recruiter interest | Interview rate  | The strongest indicator that your professional positioning is working.                                     |
| Interviews         | Offer rate      | Measures whether your interview performance and evidence match the expectations created by your portfolio. |
| Recruiter feedback | Repeated themes | The trigger for Version 1.2.                                                                               |
